Our Story

Perched high in the mountains of Batroun district, Azure by Chateau Assia was born from a passion for combining nature's beauty with culinary excellence. Our founder envisioned a place where guests could experience the magic of mountain sunsets while enjoying exceptional food and wine.

We source ingredients locally, supporting Batroun's farmers and producers, while our wine selection showcases the best of Chateau Assia's vineyards. Every dish is crafted to complement the ever-changing colors of the sunset.
