Private Villa

Our Private Villa offers the ultimate in exclusivity and comfort, featuring a private pool, direct beach access, and spacious living areas. Perfect for families or groups seeking a luxurious retreat.

Room Highlights

  • Private pool and sun deck
  • Direct beach access
  • Two king-size bedrooms
  • Spacious living and dining area
  • Fully equipped kitchen
  • Dedicated butler service

Detailed Description

At 150 square meters, the Private Villa is designed for those who desire privacy and luxury. Enjoy your own pool, a sun deck with loungers, and direct access to the pristine beach. The villa features two elegant bedrooms, each with an en-suite marble bathroom.

The spacious living and dining area is perfect for entertaining or relaxing with family and friends. A fully equipped kitchen allows for private dining experiences, and a dedicated butler is available to cater to your every need.

Floor-to-ceiling windows offer breathtaking views of the ocean and lush gardens, creating a serene and exclusive atmosphere throughout your stay.
