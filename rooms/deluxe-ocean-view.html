Deluxe Ocean View Room

Our Deluxe Ocean View rooms offer spacious comfort with private balconies showcasing breathtaking panoramic views of the ocean. Elegantly appointed with contemporary decor and premium amenities.

Room Highlights

  • Private balcony with ocean view
  • King-size bed with premium linens
  • Spacious marble bathroom
  • 55" Smart TV with streaming
  • Nespresso coffee machine
  • Twice-daily housekeeping

Detailed Description

At 45 square meters, our Deluxe Ocean View rooms are designed for both relaxation and productivity. The elegant contemporary decor features neutral tones with subtle nautical accents, creating a serene coastal ambiance.

The private balcony is furnished with comfortable seating, perfect for enjoying your morning coffee while watching the sunrise over the ocean. Floor-to-ceiling windows ensure the stunning views remain the focal point of your stay.

The marble bathroom features a walk-in rain shower, double vanity, and premium bath amenities. Plush bathrobes and slippers are provided for your comfort.
